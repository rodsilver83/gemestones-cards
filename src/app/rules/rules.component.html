How to win

There are 2 ways to win.

  • Be the first player to get 50 in your energy ( ) stockpile before anyone else.

  • Be the first player to get 3 complete Gemestones sets ( ).

Board

Sections

  • Cards: These are the cards in your hand, only visible to you. Any new cards comming form the deck will be placed here. Once a card is in play it can't go back to the cards in hand.

  • Sets: You can place cards from your hand to complete a set. Any GEMSTONE or SET that you get from other players, will be palced here.

  • Energy: Collect your ENERGY cards here, reach 50 to win. If your energy stock pile is EMPTY, you will have to pay with GEMSTONES.
